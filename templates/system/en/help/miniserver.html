IP-Address or Hostname: The IP-Address or Hostname of the Loxone Miniserver. Please ensure that the Miniserver is reachable all the time using this IP/Name.

Portnumber: The Portnumberto reach the Webinterface of the Miniservers. Usually Port 80.

Miniserver Username: Username of an Administrator-Account of the Miniserver. Often "admin".

Miniserver Password: The Password associated to the above Administrator-Account.

Note: A field for free usage. e.g. Customer name, Installation Location, ....
If you enter just an URL, an Open-Button is automatically displayed to open the URL in a new window.

Use Cloud-DNS: Tick the checkbox to use the Loxone Cloud-DNS-Service to reach the Miniserver from extern.

dns.loxonecloud.com-Address: Enter the dns.loxonecloud.com-Address for this Miniserver. Check the Loxone Homepage (Product-Registration-Area) for more.
Usually it's http://dns.loxonecloud.com/ and your Miniserver MAC-Address without separators.
